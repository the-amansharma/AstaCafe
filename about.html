Our Story

The journey from Scandinavia to your neighborhood

The Beginning

Asta Café was born from a simple idea: to create a space that embodies the Scandinavian concept of "hygge" — that feeling of coziness and contentment that comes from enjoying life's simple pleasures.

Founded in 2018 by Emma and Lars, who after years of traveling throughout Norway, Sweden, and Denmark, wanted to bring the warmth and simplicity of Nordic café culture to their hometown.

Our Philosophy

Simplicity

We believe in the beauty of simplicity — from our minimalist interior to our carefully curated menu. Every element has a purpose and a place.

Quality

We source the finest ingredients and work with local producers whenever possible. Our coffee beans are ethically sourced and roasted to perfection.

Community

Our café is more than just a place to grab coffee — it's a gathering space for the community, where connections are made and ideas are shared.

Sustainability

We're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint through compostable packaging, energy-efficient practices, and reducing waste.
